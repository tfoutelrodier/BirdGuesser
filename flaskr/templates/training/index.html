Bird Song Basics

Birds produce two types of sounds:

  • Songs: Longer, more complex vocalizations used for territory defense and mate attraction
  • Calls: Shorter, simpler vocalizations used for alarm, flight, or social contact

Most songbirds have multiple songs in their repertoire.
